This site is a project conceieved in order to hone skills, for myself as well as for you. With that in mind, here are a few goals. Because it is important to reflect and revise, items on these list must be considered ephemeral: Subject to, and due for, change at any time.

What I want to achieve:

  • Improved knowledge of Physics
  • Greater comfort with technology
  • A teaching resource I can continue using in the future
  • Example of integrating technology with education
  • Polishing of teaching methods

What I want you to achieve:

  • Physics proficiency
  • Investigative mind
  • Joy, not fear or loathing, of Physics
